### Source Quality Tiers
- **Tier 1**: Primary historical documents, field-verified GPS, professional surveys
- **Tier 2**: Academic publications, cross-referenced sources
- **Tier 3**: Secondary sources, unverified reports

We prioritize Tier 1 sources but welcome all documented contributions.
